Through my consulting services I aim to build knowledge infrastructure for sustainable production, focusing on green chemistry and material issues.

What I do

I help organizations design and adopt new approaches, systems, policies, and technologies to protect the environment and health. I also help organizations develop tools and information systems to mobilize knowledge—to inform decisions, and to understand, visualize, and communicate impacts.

How I work

My work is rooted in a strong interdisciplinary research background including chemistry, environmental, and social sciences. I combine systems thinking with technical know-how. My specialties include:

  • integrative research
  • policy analysis & development
  • data analysis & visualization
  • software & information systems design
  • technical writing
